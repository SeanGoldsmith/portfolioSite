Rubiks Cube Timer

This is an application designed to time your rubiks cube speed solves. I enjoy speedcubing and it has been a hobby of mine for about 12 years now. This timer generates a random scramble for you at the top of the screen. After applying the scramble, you hit the spacebar to begin your solve, and hit it again when you are finished. After you have complete 3,5, and 7 solves, the app will display the average of those solves.

It's important to note that in speedsolving, the fastest and slowest times are removed before calculating the average of and set 5 or higher. This is why we have a "mean" of 3 and and "average" of 5 and 7.

This app was built using html, css, and vanilla javascript. This web app is designed for desktops.
